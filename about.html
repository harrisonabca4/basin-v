About Us

Building a Pipeline from the Oil Patch to Your Portfolio

Basin Ventures is a Southlake, Texas-based energy asset management firm that provides accredited investors with direct, tax-advantaged ownership in U.S. oil and gas production through a non-operated working interest model.

Our Mission

Institutional-Grade Energy Investment, Accessible to Accredited Investors

Basin Ventures was founded on a simple premise: the most powerful wealth-building tool in oil and gas — the non-operated working interest — should not be limited to insiders, operators, and large institutions.

We serve as a capital partner and deal manager, giving investors passive exposure to domestic energy production alongside established, top-tier E&P operators. We are not an oil and gas operator. We are the bridge between proven energy assets and the investors who benefit from owning them.

Every investment opportunity we bring to market has passed our four-pillar institutional diligence framework — covering Land, Legal, Geological, and Financial analysis. Our screening process is built to find reasons to say no. Only the top fraction of deals reviewed each year are advanced.

Our Process

Four-Pillar Institutional Diligence

Every deal must pass all four pillars before a single dollar of investor capital is committed.

⚑

Land

Title and lease analysis, ownership verification, surface rights review, and chain-of-title confirmation to ensure clean, defensible interest in every asset.

⚖

Legal

Regulatory review, JOA negotiation, and contract structuring to protect investor interests. Every agreement is reviewed by energy-specialist legal counsel.

♦

Geological

Subsurface and reservoir evaluation, formation quality assessment, spacing analysis, and production potential modeling using independent petroleum engineering data.

★

Financial

Cash flow modeling, commodity price sensitivity scenarios, breakeven analysis, and IRR projection. Every deal is stress-tested before capital commitment.
