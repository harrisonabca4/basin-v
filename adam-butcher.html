Background

Adam Butcher brings more than two decades of hands-on experience in energy land brokerage, mineral rights acquisition, and oil and gas mergers and acquisitions. As President and Managing Partner of Basin Ventures, Adam leads the firm's deal sourcing, operator relationship management, and investment strategy.

Prior to co-founding Basin Ventures, Adam built and led Eagle Land Services, establishing a reputation for identifying, evaluating, and acquiring high-quality mineral and working interest positions across the nation's most productive oil and gas basins.

Over his career, Adam has personally deployed more than $500 million in mineral and working interest acquisitions, building an extensive network of operator relationships spanning more than 40 major industry players. His deep expertise in lease evaluation, title analysis, and asset valuation underpins Basin Ventures' institutional diligence framework.

Adam holds the Certified Professional Landman (CPL) designation — the highest professional credential in the energy land brokerage industry — reflecting his mastery of land practices, mineral title, and energy asset transactions.

At Basin Ventures, Adam is responsible for the four-pillar diligence process, operator selection, basin targeting, and the strategic direction of all fund vehicles. His decades of direct field experience give Basin Ventures investors access to deal flow, operator relationships, and asset evaluation capabilities that are typically reserved for large institutional players.
