Background

Tim Davis brings 20 years of legal expertise in energy contracts and regulatory compliance to Basin Ventures. As Co-Founder and Managing Partner, Tim oversees the legal structuring, regulatory compliance, and investor protection framework that underpins every Basin Ventures investment vehicle.

Tim earned his Juris Doctor from Chase College of Law and is a licensed attorney in Texas, Kentucky, and Ohio. His multi-state practice gives Basin Ventures a broad legal perspective across key energy-producing jurisdictions.

Throughout his career, Tim has specialized in the legal architecture of oil and gas transactions — from Joint Operating Agreement (JOA) negotiation and lease analysis to partnership structuring and regulatory compliance. His expertise ensures that every Basin Ventures fund is structured to maximize investor protection while preserving the full scope of tax benefits available under the Internal Revenue Code.

Tim's legal oversight spans the entire lifecycle of a Basin Ventures investment — from initial deal evaluation through the Legal pillar of the four-pillar diligence framework, to LP agreement structuring, to ongoing compliance and reporting. His focus on contract quality and regulatory rigor gives Basin Ventures investors confidence that their interests are protected at every stage.

At Basin Ventures, Tim works closely with Adam Butcher to evaluate every deal opportunity, structure fund vehicles, negotiate operator agreements, and ensure that the firm's practices meet the highest standards of legal and regulatory compliance in the energy investment space.
