Key Benefits

Why Direct Participation Matters

Offsets Active Income

Under IRC §469(c)(3), working interest deductions offset active ordinary income — including W-2 wages, business income, and professional earnings. This is unavailable through stocks, ETFs, or REITs.

Year 1 Deductibility

Up to 100% of your investment may be deductible in the year it is made through the combination of IDC deductions and TDC bonus depreciation — providing immediate tax relief.

Ongoing Tax-Free Income

The 15% percentage depletion allowance means a portion of your production income is tax-free every year for the life of the asset — regardless of your original cost basis.
